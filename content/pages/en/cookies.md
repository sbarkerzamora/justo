# Cookies Policy

**Last updated:** June 3, 2026

---

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store in your browser to remember information about your visit. They can be first-party (from the site you are visiting) or third-party.

## 2. Cookies Used by Justo

Justo **does not use cookies** for tracking, advertising, personalization, or analytics. All application functionality operates without tracking cookies.

## 3. Local Storage

Instead of cookies, Justo uses browser `localStorage` to remember two preferences:

- **Selected country**: to show the appropriate country information and tools upon return
- **Preferred language**: to maintain your language setting between visits

This data:
- Remains exclusively on your device
- Is not accessible by third parties
- Is not transmitted to our servers
- Can be deleted from your browser settings at any time

## 4. Analytics (Plausible)

We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly tool that **does not use cookies** and respects user privacy. Collected data is anonymous and aggregated, without identifying individual users.

## 5. Third-Party Cookies

Justo does not incorporate third-party cookies, advertising networks, or social media services that install cookies.

## 6. How to Manage Local Storage

You can delete or block local storage from your browser settings:

- **Chrome**: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox**: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari**: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
- **Edge**: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Storage

## 7. Updates

We may update this policy when necessary. The date of the latest update appears at the top of the document.
