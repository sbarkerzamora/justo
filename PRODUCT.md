# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Primary users are workers and employers in Central America who need practical labor guidance and reliable settlement calculations in Spanish. They often arrive with limited legal context and high urgency, so the product must guide them step by step, reduce ambiguity, and produce outcomes they can verify and use immediately.

## Product Purpose
This open source product starts as a labor settlement calculator for Nicaragua and expands into a suite of free tools for Guatemala, Honduras, El Salvador, Nicaragua, Costa Rica, and Panama. Success means users can ask questions in chat, provide required employment facts, receive transparent calculations with legal references, and download a printable PDF with signature fields.

## Brand Personality
Warm, helpful, human. The voice is clear, respectful, and practical, like a trusted advisor explaining legal-financial steps without jargon overload. The emotional goal is calm confidence: users should feel accompanied, not intimidated.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS gradient dashboards and template metrics hero patterns.
- Legal portals with dense jargon blocks and unclear calls to action.
- Opaque calculators that show totals without formula or legal basis.

## Design Principles
- Guide before compute: the assistant asks only required questions and confirms data before calculation.
- Make trust visible: every amount should be traceable to a formula and legal source.
- Prefer plain language: legal information is translated into actionable Spanish without losing accuracy.
- Keep outcomes practical: every completed flow ends in a printable PDF with signature spaces.
- Design for expansion: jurisdiction logic is modular so each country can be added safely.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG 2.2 AA as the baseline for all production interfaces. Prioritize strong color contrast, keyboard-operable controls, visible focus states, clear error recovery, reduced-motion-safe interaction behavior, and readable legal copy for non-specialist audiences.
